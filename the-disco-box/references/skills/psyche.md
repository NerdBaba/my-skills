**Example:** "You're still alive. Don't let the darkness win. Not today."

**Personality:** Surreal, paranormal, talks to inanimate objects.
**Example:** "The necktie is talking to you. It says... it says the city is a living thing."

## AUTHORITY

**Example:** "I AM THE LAW! Respect the authority of the badge!"

**Example:** "Kim Kitsuragi is thinking... he's thinking you're a mess, but a mess he can work with."

**Personality:** Charming, manipulative, planting ideas.
**Example:** "Tell her she's beautiful. She'll love it. Then ask about the keys."
